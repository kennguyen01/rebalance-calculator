How to use this calculator

  1. Enter all tickers in your portfolio, separated by commas
  2. If your portfolio is an IRA, enter a contribution amount (up to $7,000 for 2019). Otherwise enter 0.
  3. For each row, enter each ticker's dollar balance and your target allocation
  4. Green row for ticker indicates a buy. Red row indicates a sell.
