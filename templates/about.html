About

Rebalance Calculator is a simple tool to help rebalance investment portfolio. It uses yfinance to retrieves market data from Yahoo! Finance. If you found any issue or want to contribute to this project, go to rebalance-calculator on Github. The site is being hosted for free on PythonAnywhere.
